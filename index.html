Maggi

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter (optional)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • Maggi tastemaker

Steps:

  1. Heat oil or butter in a pan.
  2. Add chopped onion, tomato, and vegetables. Sauté for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Pour 1 cup of water into the pan and bring it to a boil.
  4. Add the Maggi tastemaker (masala) and mix well.
  5. Break the Maggi noodles into halves and add them to the boiling water.
  6. Cook on medium flame for 2–3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. When the water is absorbed and noodles are soft, turn off the flame.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Maggi!
